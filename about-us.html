EST. 1924
The Genesis

A Legacy Carved in 24-Karat Gold

Founded in the heart of Pochampally's jewellery district, Trilok began as a humble atelier with a single purpose: to elevate the traditional art of temple jewelry. Our founder, Om Sri, believed that every piece of jewellery not only carries wealth but a real-time section, ineffable story crafted to outlast generations.

"Our vision is to bridge the chasm between ancient artisan mastery and the digital future of luxury."
