Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Trilok Chit Plan

Can I enrol in multiple chit plans simultaneously? +

Yes, you can enrol in multiple plans at different amounts simultaneously. Each plan is tracked independently and you earn a separate bonus for each.

What if I miss a monthly payment? +

You have a 7-day grace period for each instalment. If you miss more than one instalment, the plan is paused and you can resume within 30 days with no penalty.

Can I redeem my plan value for any jewellery? +

Absolutely. The total value including the bonus can be redeemed towards any jewellery on our website or in our stores — rings, necklaces, bangles, and more.

Is there a making charge on redemption? +

Standard making charges apply on the redeemed jewellery, but the metal value and stone value are fully covered by your chit plan amount.

Can I withdraw my money if I change my mind? +

You can exit the plan before completion and receive all your paid instalments back in full. The bonus amount applies only on completion of the 10-month term.

How do I enrol in the Trilok Chit Plan? +

Simply click "Enrol Now" on your chosen plan, create an account or sign in, set up your monthly payment method, and you're enrolled within 5 minutes.
