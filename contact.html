Customer Care

Common Enquiries

Quick answers to our most frequent customer questions.

What is the standard delivery timeline?
⌄

For certified gold and diamond jewelry, we typically ship within 3–5 business days. Bespoke bridal sets may require 4–6 weeks for artisan craftsmanship and hallmarking. All shipments are fully insured and require a signature upon delivery.

What is your return & exchange policy?
⌄

We offer a 7-day return window for all standard products in original, unworn condition with all certificates intact. Custom and bespoke orders are non-returnable but eligible for modification within 30 days of delivery.

How do I determine my ring or bangle size?
⌄

You can use our free Ring Size Guide available in the Customer Care section, or visit any Trilok showroom for a complimentary professional sizing. We also offer a free resize service within 60 days of purchase.

Are all products BIS Hallmarked?
⌄

Yes, 100% of our gold jewellery carries the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) hallmark, certifying purity and fineness. Each piece is accompanied by a detailed certificate of authenticity.
