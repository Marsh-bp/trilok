Common questions about the Ethereal Collection

How do I verify the gold authenticity?
Every Trilok piece carries a BIS Hallmark certification confirming metal purity. Additionally, you'll receive a quality assurance card and independent certification. You can also get any piece tested at a certified assay centre — we actively encourage this.
Is it safe to order luxury jewellery online?
Absolutely. All orders are fully insured during transit, shipped in tamper-evident packaging, and require signature upon delivery. We partner only with premium logistics providers and offer a 30-day return policy with no questions asked.
Can I customise the metal colour?
Yes — this design is available in Yellow Gold, Rose Gold, White Gold, and Platinum. Select your preferred purity and metal using the options above, or reach out to our concierge team for bespoke customisation requests including engraving and stone upgrades.
